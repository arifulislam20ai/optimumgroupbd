Optimum Group Limited

Company Profile

Optimum Group Limited is a diversified Bangladesh-based business conglomerate operating across Real Estate, Hospitality, Food & Beverage, Healthcare, Education, and Agro industries. The company is committed to delivering quality services, developing sustainable communities, and expanding its business both locally and internationally, with a strong focus on innovation and customer satisfaction.

Mission

To provide high-quality, reliable, and innovative products and services that enhance the lifestyle of our customers while contributing to sustainable economic growth.

Vision

To become a trusted global business group by establishing a strong presence in both domestic and international markets, especially in real estate and hospitality sectors.

Message from the Chairman

At Optimum Group Limited, we are driven by a vision to build a sustainable and diversified business that contributes to economic growth and social development. Our commitment is to uphold integrity, quality, and long-term value in every sector we operate. We believe in creating opportunities, empowering communities, and expanding our presence both in Bangladesh and globally.

Optimum Builders

Company Profile

Optimum Builders is the real estate wing of Optimum Group Limited, dedicated to delivering modern, sustainable, and affordable housing solutions across Bangladesh. The company focuses on residential and commercial development, ensuring quality construction, innovative design, and customer satisfaction in both urban and rural areas.

Mission

To develop high-quality, safe, and sustainable real estate projects that enhance living standards and create long-term value for customers and communities.

Vision

To become a trusted and leading real estate brand in Bangladesh by providing reliable, innovative, and customer-focused housing solutions.

Optimum Agro

Company Profile

Optimum Agro, a concern of Optimum Group Limited, is a large-scale agro-based initiative focused on modern, sustainable, and technology-driven agriculture in Bangladesh. The company is engaged in farming, agro-processing, and supply chain development to ensure safe, high-quality food production while supporting local farmers and improving agricultural efficiency.

Mission

To produce safe, high-quality agricultural products through modern farming techniques while supporting farmers and promoting sustainable agriculture.

Vision

To become a trusted agro brand in Bangladesh and expand into international markets with innovative and environmentally responsible solutions.
